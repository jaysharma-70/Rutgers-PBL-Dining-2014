Dining with the Distinguished

This is a night of banquet style food and networking between ambitious students and business professionals, taking place on Friday, December 5th at Rutgers around 6:00pm. Any student interested in career advice can attend for the opportunity to network with distinguished representatives from a variety of companies. Last year, we had 120 students and 17 professionals in attendance.

Where: Rutgers New Brunswick, Livingston Campus

When: December 5, 2014 6PM
